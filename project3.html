About AlgoPrep

AlgoPrep is an online learning platform founded in 2023 by Nishant Chahar and Subhesh Kumar, aimed at helping students and professionals enhance their programming skills. It offers a range of courses, including Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), Development, and System Design, with expert-led instruction, personalized learning paths, and hands-on coding exercises. The flagship program, AlgoPrepX, provides over 450 hours of content covering DSA, Development, Low-Level Design (LLD), Core Subjects, and Prompt Engineering. With industry-experienced instructors like Nishant Chahar (ex-Microsoft) and Subhesh Kumar (Google), the platform has helped numerous learners secure jobs at top companies. Students praise AlgoPrep for its structured curriculum, real-world projects, and strong community support.
